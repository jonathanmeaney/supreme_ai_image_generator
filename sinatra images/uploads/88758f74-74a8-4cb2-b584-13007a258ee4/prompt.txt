In a lavish ballroom, the Tea Mistress stands elegantly among the crowd, a majestic figure cloaked in luxurious fur and wearing aubergine-colored gown that shimmers under the chandeliers. Her fingertips, perfectly manicured, deftly hold a Toblerone, a unique choice for such an occasion. Curiously, her feet are clad in silver flippers, an unexpected accessory that catches the eye. As she glides through the dancefloor with grace, it becomes evident that beneath her gown, cloven hooves peek out, adding an enchanting mystery to her presence. The scene is a delightful blend of extravagance and whimsy, leaving all who witness it captivated by the enigmatic Tea Mistress.
