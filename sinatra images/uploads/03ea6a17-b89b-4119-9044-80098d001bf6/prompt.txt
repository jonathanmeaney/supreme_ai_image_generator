In a post-apocalyptic world, a peculiar sight awaits you: an electric beggar with fur-covered skin sits at the entrance of a 24-hour abattoir. Clutching a broom handle like a staff, the beggar's eyes flicker with a strange electricity. Nearby, an old shotgun leans against the wall, hinting at the beggar's resourcefulness for survival in this brutal world. The scene is eerie yet intriguing, inviting you to discover the beggar's story and the secrets hidden within the abattoir.
