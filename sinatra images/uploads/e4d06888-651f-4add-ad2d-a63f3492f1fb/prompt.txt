In a whimsical dystopian world, a motorized chairperson with sleek fins glides through the bustling streets. Beside them, a crooked editrix with glass eyes gazes upon the chaos with a calculating stare. The backdrop is a 24-hour abattoir, its eerie glow illuminating the darkened cityscape. Suddenly, a rogue chocolate Easter bunny hops by, followed closely by a figure wielding a sledgehammer. Among the scattered debris lies a mysterious bag of teeth, hinting at secrets yet to be uncovered. What strange tale lies behind this bizarre convergence of characters and objects?
